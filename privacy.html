Privacy Policy

App: Anime Watchlist & Tracker
Last updated: 17 January 2026

Summary

  • No account required.
  • No personal information is collected by the developer.
  • Your watchlist is stored locally on your device only.
  • Anime data is fetched from the AniList API (https://graphql.anilist.co).

Data we collect

We do not collect personal data.

Your watchlist is saved locally on your device (on-device storage).

Third-party services

The app requests anime information from AniList. Trailer links may open YouTube.
